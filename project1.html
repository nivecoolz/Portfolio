Healthcare Analytics

Involved analyzing patient wait times to identify inefficiencies in the healthcare system. Large datasets were processed using SQL to extract relevant information from hospital systems. DAX was employed within Power BI to create interactive dashboards that tracked wait time metrics in real-time. Regression models were applied to determine the factors contributing to delays, helping to optimize operations. The analysis resulted in a 15% reduction in average wait times, improving both efficiency and patient satisfaction.

Technologies Used: SQL, Power BI, DAX, Regression Models
